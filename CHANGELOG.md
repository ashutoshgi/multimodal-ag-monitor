# Changelog

## v0.1.0 - 2026-06-08

Initial public-ready release.

- Config-driven field setup.
- Sentinel-2 and Sentinel-1 download workflow.
- Sentinel-2 vegetation-index time-series extraction and QC.
- Sentinel-1 VV/VH time-series extraction and QC.
- Landsat NDVI time-series extraction and QC.
- gridMET, CHIRPS, and ERA5-Land weather time-series extraction.
- Multimodal table generation and plotting.
- Optional crop-mask support.
- KMZ/KML to shapefile batch conversion utility.
