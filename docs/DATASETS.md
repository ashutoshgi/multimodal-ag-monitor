# Datasets

This workflow uses publicly available Earth observation and weather products accessible through Google Earth Engine.

## Sentinel-2 Surface Reflectance

Used for optical vegetation indices including NDVI, SAVI, NDWI, and NDRE. These indices describe canopy greenness, vegetation vigor, water-related spectral response, and seasonal crop development.

## Sentinel-1 GRD SAR

Used for VV and VH C-band SAR backscatter and derived metrics such as VV−VH, VH−VV, VV/VH, and VH/VV. SAR is useful because it can provide cloud-independent information related to crop structure, surface roughness, and moisture conditions.

## Landsat Surface Reflectance

Used for long-term NDVI time series. Landsat provides historical context and supports interannual field behavior analysis.

## gridMET

Used for daily meteorological variables such as precipitation, reference evapotranspiration, and vapor pressure deficit.

## CHIRPS

Used as an independent precipitation data source.

## ERA5-Land

Used for reanalysis-based environmental variables such as temperature, precipitation, and soil moisture-related variables.
